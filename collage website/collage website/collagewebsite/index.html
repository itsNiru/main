WELCOME TO KLNCE

Founded in 1994, K.L.N. College of Engineering is Madurai's first self-financing Co-educational Engineering College. Sponsored by industrialists and academicians, it shifted to its own 53.8-acre campus in 1996. The current administration, led by Dr. K.N.K. Ganesh, oversees ongoing construction projects for infrastructure enhancement. Facilities include well-equipped classrooms, drawing halls, seminar halls, and an air-conditioned computer center. The campus features laboratory buildings, workshops, and an automobile workshop. Accommodation facilities comprise men's hostels, a mess block, and a canteen block, fostering a conducive study environment. Recent developments include ongoing construction for the Mechanical and Automobile departments, extensions for Electrical & Electronics Engineering and Administrative South Block, and a building for a generator installation. The college's commitment to academic excellence is evident in the installation of 950 computers and ongoing efforts to provide essential infrastructure.
